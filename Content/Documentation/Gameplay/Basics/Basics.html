Combat Basics

Controls
AHFG can be controlled with two input methods: keyboard or mouse.

Note that the keybinds listed are the defaults and can be changed if you so wish
Basic actions

Moving: To walk/run left or right, use the A and D keys on keyboard and tilt the left control stick on gamepad.

Jumping: To jump, press W on keyboard or the bottom circular button on gamepad.
You can press the jump button again to double jump, and you can influence the direction you fall by pressing left or right.

Normal Attack: On keyboard, use the P key to use a normal attack or the right circular button on gamepad.
You can use different attacks called "tilt attacks" by pressing the normal attack button while holding a direction.

Aerial Attack: If you press the attack button while airborne, you will use an aerial attack. Aerial attacks can be directional too!

Finisher Attack: If you double tap a direction or flick the control stick very hard and then press the attack button, you will use a very powerful finisher move.
If your timing is off, it may not work - try to attack slightly after you hit the direction!

Special Attack: On keyboard, use the O key to use a special attack or the left circular button on gamepad.
You have four special attacks, which you can choose by holding a direction while you press the button.
Special attacks often have the most unique effects.
